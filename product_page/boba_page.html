Boba Brews: Your Gateway to Flavorful Delights

  • Origin

    Boba tea, also known as bubble tea, originated in Taiwan in the 1980s.

  • Ingredients

    It typically consists of black or green tea, milk (or non-dairy alternatives), sugar syrup, and chewy tapioca pearls.

  • Variety of Flavors

    Boba tea comes in a wide range of flavors, from traditional milk tea to fruit-based options, allowing for a diverse and customizable experience.

  • Chewy Tapioca Pearls

    Tapioca pearls, also known as "boba," are a signature component of this beverage, providing a unique texture and flavor. They are often found at the bottom of the drink.
