About Me

My name is Christina Gao and I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. I attended the University of California at Santa Cruz (2019-2023) and obtained a Bachelors of Science in Technology Information Management and a Minor in Economics.

I developed an interest in data analysis when I was introduced to SQL during one of my undergraduate courses, CSE182: Introduction to Database Management Systems.

What intrigues me the most about data is its power to unlock valuable insights and drive informed decision-making. Data serves as a gateway to understanding the world around us, and it allows us to uncover trends, solve complex problems, and make predictions. The ability to gather, analyze, and interpret data has the potential to transform industries, improve processes, and enhance our understanding of various phenomena.

September 11, 2023: I earned the Responsive Web Design certification from freeCodeCamp, which equipped me with the HTML and CSS skills necessary to create this personalized portfolio!

September 27, 2023: I completed the Data Analysis with Python certification on freeCodeCamp. This achievement allowed me to refine my Python skills, including proficiency with libraries like Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

In Progress: I am currently working on the Relational Database certification from freeCodeCamp in order to improve my SQL and Bash skills.
